About

Welcome to the Rayna Latin Dance Club — a friendly community learning and enjoying Latin and social dances. Whether you’re brand new or returning to dance, you’ll find clear instruction and a welcoming floor.

  • Small group classes with step-by-step guidance
  • Social practice with curated music
  • Special workshops and community events
